# Grand Plaza Rewards Loyalty Program Policy

**Last updated:** February 2024  
**Owner:** Marketing & Guest Services  
**Document ID:** POL-MKT-2024-002

## Program Overview

The Grand Plaza Rewards loyalty program is designed to recognize and reward our most valued guests who choose to stay with us repeatedly. The program operates on a tiered structure with three membership levels: Gold, Platinum, and Diamond, each offering progressively enhanced benefits and privileges. Membership in the program is complimentary and open to all guests aged 18 and older. The program is designed to provide meaningful value to frequent travelers while encouraging continued loyalty to the Grand Plaza Hotel brand. Members earn points for eligible stays and can redeem those points for free nights, room upgrades, dining credits, spa services, and other rewards. Beyond points earning and redemption, each membership tier provides a suite of benefits including room upgrades, late checkout, bonus points, and exclusive member rates that are designed to enhance the overall guest experience and provide tangible value that increases with the member's level of engagement with the brand.

---

## Membership Tiers and Qualification

The Grand Plaza Rewards program features three membership tiers, each with specific qualification requirements based on annual stay activity. Gold Status is the entry tier and is achieved by completing 25 qualifying nights or 10 qualifying stays within a calendar year. Platinum Status, the mid-tier level, requires 50 qualifying nights or 20 qualifying stays within a calendar year. Diamond Status, the highest tier, requires 75 qualifying nights or 30 qualifying stays within a calendar year. Qualifying nights are defined as nights for which the member was the registered guest on a paid reservation, excluding complimentary stays, reward night redemptions, and stays booked with third-party rates that do not earn points. Qualifying stays are defined as separate reservations, regardless of the number of nights, that meet the qualifying night criteria. For example, a member who completes 10 separate weekend stays of two nights each would have 20 qualifying nights and 10 qualifying stays, which would qualify them for Gold Status.

Tier status is evaluated on a calendar year basis from January 1 through December 31, and members must re-qualify each year to maintain their status level. However, once a member achieves a tier level, that status is maintained through the end of the following calendar year, providing a grace period for re-qualification. For example, a member who achieves Platinum Status in June 2024 will maintain Platinum Status through December 31, 2025, regardless of their stay activity during that period, although they would need to re-qualify during 2025 to maintain Platinum Status into 2026. This structure provides members with a full year to enjoy their earned benefits while working toward re-qualification. New members who join the program start at the base membership level with no tier status, but they immediately begin earning credit toward tier qualification from their first eligible stay.

## Points Earning Structure

Members earn points for eligible stays based on the amount spent on qualifying charges. The base earning rate is 10 points per dollar spent on room rate and applicable taxes. Gold Status members earn a 25% bonus on base points, effectively earning 12.5 points per dollar. Platinum Status members earn a 50% bonus, earning 15 points per dollar. Diamond Status members earn a 75% bonus, earning 17.5 points per dollar. These bonus multipliers apply automatically to all eligible stays once the member has achieved the respective tier status. Points are calculated based on the room rate and taxes only; incidental charges such as dining, spa services, parking, and other amenities do not earn points unless specifically included in a package rate.

Points are posted to the member's account within 5 to 7 business days after checkout, although during peak periods this timeline may extend to 10 business days. Members can view their points balance and transaction history by logging into their account on the hotel's website or mobile app. Points earned through stays are considered base points, but members can also earn bonus points through promotional offers, special packages, and partner activities. The hotel regularly offers double points promotions, bonus points for stays during specific periods, and targeted offers to individual members based on their stay patterns and preferences. These promotional points are subject to the specific terms of each promotion and may have different posting timelines than base points earned from stays.

## Points Redemption Options

Members can redeem accumulated points for a variety of rewards, with free night stays being the most popular redemption option. Standard room free nights are available starting at 10,000 points per night, although this rate varies based on the season and demand. During peak periods, free night redemptions may require 15,000 to 20,000 points per night, while during low-demand periods, special redemption rates as low as 7,500 points per night may be available. Deluxe room free nights typically require 15,000 to 25,000 points per night, and suite redemptions range from 30,000 to 50,000 points per night depending on the suite category and season. Members can view current redemption rates by checking availability on the website or by contacting the reservations team.

Beyond free night stays, members can redeem points for room upgrades at a rate of 5,000 points per category upgrade per night, subject to availability. Dining credits are available at 2,500 points for a $25 credit, 5,000 points for a $50 credit, or 10,000 points for a $100 credit, which can be used at any of the hotel's restaurants or for in-room dining. Spa service credits follow a similar structure, with 5,000 points providing a $50 credit toward spa treatments. Members can also redeem points for late checkout extensions, with 2,500 points providing late checkout until 2:00 PM, subject to availability. Points can be combined with cash for redemptions, allowing members to use points to offset part of the cost of a stay or service while paying the remainder in cash. This flexibility is particularly useful for members who want to red

eem rewards but don't have quite enough points for a full redemption.

Points do not expire as long as the member has qualifying account activity at least once every 24 months. Qualifying activity includes earning points from a stay, redeeming points for a reward, or purchasing points through the program's points purchase option. If an account remains inactive for 24 consecutive months, all points in the account will expire and cannot be reinstated. The hotel sends reminder emails to members approaching the 24-month inactivity threshold, encouraging them to book a stay or engage with the program to maintain their points balance.

## Tier Benefits and Privileges

Each membership tier provides a suite of benefits designed to enhance the guest experience and provide value beyond points earning and redemption. Gold Status members receive priority late checkout until 12:00 PM subject to availability, a welcome amenity upon arrival such as a fruit plate or bottle of water, access to exclusive member rates that are typically 5-10% below standard rates, and one complimentary room upgrade certificate per year that can be applied to a reservation for a one-category upgrade subject to availability. Gold members also receive priority consideration for room upgrades at check-in when higher category rooms are available, although upgrades are not guaranteed.

Platinum Status members receive all Gold benefits plus enhanced privileges including guaranteed late checkout until 1:00 PM, a more substantial welcome amenity such as a bottle of wine or chocolate amenity, access to the executive lounge when available at the property, two complimentary room upgrade certificates per year, and priority consideration for suite upgrades when available. Platinum members also receive complimentary high-speed internet in their rooms, although this benefit is increasingly standard for all guests at many properties. Platinum members are given priority for room assignment, often receiving rooms on higher floors or with better views when available.

Diamond Status members receive all Platinum benefits plus the highest level of privileges including guaranteed late checkout until 2:00 PM, a premium welcome amenity, guaranteed executive lounge access, four complimentary room upgrade certificates per year, confirmed suite upgrades when booking at least 5 days in advance on eligible rates, complimentary breakfast for up to two people daily, and a dedicated Diamond member services phone line with priority customer service. Diamond members also receive guaranteed room availability when booking at least 48 hours in advance, meaning the hotel will always have a room available for Diamond members even during sold-out periods, although this may occasionally require the hotel to walk another guest to accommodate the Diamond member. Diamond members are the hotel's most valued guests and staff are trained to provide exceptional service and recognition to these members throughout their stay.

## Member Rates and Exclusive Offers

All loyalty program members have access to exclusive member rates that are not available to the general public. These rates are typically 5-15% below standard flexible rates and are available for booking on the hotel's website and through the reservations center when the member is logged into their account. Member rates are subject to availability and may have limited inventory during peak periods, but the hotel makes efforts to maintain reasonable availability of member rates throughout the year. Member rates are flexible and follow the standard cancellation policy, providing members with both value and flexibility.

In addition to standard member rates, the hotel regularly offers targeted promotions to loyalty program members. These may include bonus points offers, discounted rates for specific travel periods, package deals that combine room rates with dining or spa credits, and last-minute deals for upcoming availability. Members receive these offers via email based on their communication preferences and can also view current offers by logging into their account on the website. The frequency and nature of offers may vary based on the member's tier status and stay history, with higher-tier members and more frequent guests often receiving more personalized and valuable offers.

## Family and Friends Benefits

Loyalty program members can extend certain benefits to family members and friends traveling with them or booking separate reservations. When a member books a reservation for another person, that reservation will earn points to the member's account as long as the member's loyalty number is included in the reservation and the member is paying for the stay. However, tier benefits such as room upgrades, late checkout, and lounge access apply only to the member's own room and do not automatically extend to rooms booked for others. The exception is when family members are staying in the same room as the member, in which case all benefits apply to everyone in that room.

Members can designate one household member to share their account, allowing that person to earn and redeem points from the same account balance. This is particularly useful for couples or family members who travel together frequently. The designated household member must be added to the account through the member's online profile, and both individuals can then earn and redeem points from the shared balance. However, tier status qualification is based on the combined stay activity of both individuals, and tier benefits apply to both when traveling separately. This shared account structure recognizes that many households make travel decisions together and allows families to pool their loyalty benefits.

## Points Purchase and Transfer

Members have the option to purchase additional points if they need to top up their balance for a specific redemption. Points can be purchased at a rate of $0.01 per point, with a minimum purchase of 1,000 points and a maximum purchase of 50,000 points per calendar year. Purchased points are posted to the account immediately and can be used for any redemption option. While purchasing points can be convenient for members who are just short of a redemption threshold, it's important to note that purchasing points is generally less cost-effective than earning points through stays, and members should carefully consider whether purchasing points provides good value for their specific situation.

Points transfers between member accounts are permitted in limited circumstances. Members can transfer points to another member's account at a rate of 1,000 points per transfer, with a minimum transfer of 5,000 points and a maximum of 50,000 points per calendar year. A transfer fee of $10 per 1,000 points transferred applies. Points transfers are processed within 3-5 business days and are irreversible once completed. The hotel allows points transfers to provide flexibility for members who want to help family members or friends reach a redemption goal, but the transfer fees are designed to discourage speculative point trading or commercial point selling.

## Program Terms and Conditions

Membership in the Grand Plaza Rewards program is subject to the program's terms and conditions, which are available in full on the hotel's website. Key terms include that membership is personal and non-transferable except for the household member sharing provision described above, that the hotel reserves the right to modify program benefits, earning rates, and redemption rates with 30 days notice to members, that the hotel may terminate membership for fraudulent activity or abuse of program benefits, and that points have no cash value and cannot be sold or bartered. Members are responsible for maintaining accurate contact information in their profile to ensure they receive important program communications and promotional offers.

The hotel takes program integrity seriously and investigates any suspected fraudulent activity or gaming of the program. Examples of prohibited activities include creating multiple accounts to earn duplicate benefits, booking stays with no intention of actually staying to earn points, claiming points for stays that were not actually completed, or attempting to manipulate tier qualification through artificial stay patterns. Members found to be engaging in such activities may have their accounts suspended or terminated and may forfeit all accumulated points and tier status. The hotel's decision regarding program violations is final and not subject to appeal.

## Customer Service and Support

Members who have questions about their account, points balance, tier status, or program benefits can contact the loyalty program customer service team through several channels. The dedicated member services phone line is available 24/7 and provides priority service to Platinum and Diamond members. Members can also email the loyalty program team at rewards@grandplazahotel.com or use the live chat feature on the website when logged into their account. Response times for email inquiries are typically within 24 hours, and the team strives to resolve most issues on the first contact.

Common member inquiries include questions about missing points from recent stays, tier qualification status, redemption options and availability, and how to maximize program benefits. The customer service team is trained to provide detailed information about the program and to assist members with booking reward stays, processing points purchases or transfers, and resolving any issues with their account. Members are encouraged to contact customer service promptly if they notice any discrepancies in their account or if they have questions about how to best utilize their membership benefits.
